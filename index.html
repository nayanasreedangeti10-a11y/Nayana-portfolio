The Creator Behind NEZOR

Hi, I'm Nayana Sree.

I'm the creative force behind NEZOR — a one-person cinematic studio built on the belief that every frame should have a reason to exist. As a self-taught video editor and visual storyteller based in India, I don't just cut clips — I engineer emotional experiences. Each project I take on is obsessed over, frame by frame, beat by beat, until the story breathes on its own.

My editing philosophy is simple: precision kills mediocrity. Whether it's a 15-second reel that stops the scroll or a full cinematic sequence that leaves you speechless — I bring the same ruthless attention to pacing, color, and sound. NEZOR is not an agency. It's a vision. My vision.

1. Consultation

Defining narrative goals, storyboard pacing, music direction, references, and overall brand identity alignment.

2. Footage Review

Detailed curation, quality verification, cataloging cameras/framerates, and structuring assets for efficient workflow.

3. Core Editing

Rough cut assembly, synchronization, high-impact pacing edits, transitions, motion graphics, and basic VFX overlays.

4. Revisions

Collaborative fine-tuning, audio leveling updates, client feedback incorporation, and frame-by-frame review.

5. Delivery

Rendering in raw cinematic codecs (ProRes/DNxHR/H.264) optimized for web, theatrical, or broadcast delivery.
